Instructions for Carroll et al. 2020 (JAMES) Green's Functions Experiments


**Initial Condition Experiments**

Initial biogeochemical conditions for Experiments #2–11 (Table 1) and Experiments #L1–4 (Table S1)
are located at the ECCO Data Portal, https://data.nas.nasa.gov/ecco/

under:
eccodata/llc_270/ecco_darwin_v4/input/darwin_initial_conditions


**Parameter Sensitivity Experiments**

For Experiment #18 (optimized simulation), use the values in code_darwin/
For Experiments #2–11 and #L1–L3, use the Brix et al. (2015) parameter values shown in Table 1. 

For Experiments #12–17 and #L4, adjust parameters below to Brix et al. (2015) values 
+ the perturbation listed in the experiment description 
(Table 1 column B, and Table S1 2nd column):

Iron dust solubility: variable alpfe on L83 in code_darwin/darwin_init_fixed.F  
Iron scavenging rate: variable scav_rat on L101 in code_darwin/darwin_init_fixed.F  
Small phytoplankton growth rate: variable Smallgrow on L161 in code_darwin/darwin_init_fixed.F  
Large phytoplankton growth rate: variable Biggrow on L161 in code_darwin/darwin_init_fixed.F 
Diatom palatability: variable diatomgraz on L272 in code_darwin/darwin_init_fixed.F  
PIC/POC ratio: variable R_PICPOC on L484 in code_darwin/darwin_generate_phyto.F
